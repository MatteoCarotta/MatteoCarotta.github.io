Pricing

a. Serching Enterprises pay once they use our space-modeller, they get access to the dashboard and they pay depending on the number of their employees (number of people doing the test) + the space-modeller recieves more value + the companies get something right away when they pay (they don't feel like they just pay a fee to be on the platform without finding a space for example)

b. The owners of the spaces pay a transaction fee once a deal is done If a real estate agency is our customer it should be less than administrative costs if they were not using our platform in case of a private owner it should be less than the price of a real estate agency + we can offer a service that is cheaper than a real estate agency, because we digitalize part of their process
